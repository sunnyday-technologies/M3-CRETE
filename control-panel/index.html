Interactive Planner // Power & Control Enclosure

Control Panel Layout OptimizerNew

Plan the M3-CRETE power and control box visually. Drag DIN-rail devices, PSUs and bus bars to scale; the tool routes the wiring orthogonally and tracks total wire length live. Hit Optimize to let it slide and rotate parts toward the shortest, cleanest layout — then add your own modules (BuckPucks, DAC/ADC converters, anything) with custom inputs and outputs.

Drag to move · select a part + R to rotate · tap terminal → terminal to wire · Del to remove · Save/Load keeps your layout as JSON · Open full-screen ↗ (best on a tablet or desktop for fine wiring)

Optimize for short runs

Simulated-annealing search slides and rotates parts to minimize total routed wire length while keeping devices on the rail and off each other — press it a few times and keep the best.

Custom modules

Add any part by label and X-Y size, then list its inputs and outputs. Pins drop onto the body (inputs left, outputs right) ready to wire. Built for the add-ons — LED BuckPucks, digital-to-analog converters, sensor boards.

Save & share

Export the whole layout — parts, positions, rotations and the netlist — to a JSON file, and load it back later. Bring your own enclosure plan or fork the default M3-CRETE build.

Qualified technical use — planning tool only

This is a layout and visualization aid for qualified technical users; it is not a wiring diagram, a certified schematic, or AC mains wiring instructions. Any AC mains wiring, grounding, disconnects, emergency-stop power circuits, enclosure integration, and code-compliance work must be performed by qualified personnel — a licensed electrician where required, or a qualified control-panel shop. Verify every connection, rating, and clearance against the actual components and applicable code before building. Use at your own risk under CERN-OHL-W-2.0.
