# M3-CRETE Electrical Scope Boundary

The public M3-CRETE release is limited to mechanical design information,
low-voltage control-interface references, and software/firmware references
where published. The public release does not include AC mains design, 48 VDC or
other high-current power-distribution design, safety-rated control design,
emergency-stop power-circuit design, control-panel construction, facility
wiring, or UL/NRTL/AHJ approval.

Any certified, listed, field-evaluated, or inspected power/control enclosure is
a separate professional scope and applies only to the exact configuration
evaluated. Certification of a power/control enclosure does not certify the
complete M3-CRETE machine, installation, printed output, site, operating
procedure, or downstream modifications.

## Public content allowed

- Mechanical design references.
- Low-voltage interface boundaries where values are reviewed and marked.
- Software and firmware references where published.
- Warnings that identify professional electrical, control-panel, safety, and
  authority-review responsibilities.

## Public content not provided

- AC line, neutral, or protective-earth wiring.
- Power-supply primary-side wiring.
- Branch-circuit, breaker, contactor, disconnect, or facility-connection
  wiring.
- Safety-rated emergency-stop power-circuit implementation.
- 48 VDC or other high-current power-distribution design.
- Control-panel construction drawings or netlists.
- Claims that any panel mark certifies the whole machine or installation.

## Professional review required

Before energizing or operating any M3-CRETE-derived system, qualified
professionals must review and accept the electrical power system, controls,
machine guarding, emergency-stop behavior, grounding/bonding, overcurrent
protection, facility connection, inspection basis, and operating procedures for
the actual installation and intended environment.
