# M3-CRETE Safety Notice

## Experimental open-hardware reference design, not a certified product

M3-CRETE is not a consumer product, not a hobby kit, not a finished machine,
not a certified construction printer, and not a structural concrete production
system. It is an experimental reference design for qualified technical teams
working in controlled R&D, laboratory, or supervised institutional settings.

Sunnyday Technologies does not provide public AC mains wiring instructions,
control-panel build instructions, facility-connection instructions,
safety-rated emergency-stop circuit designs, or code-compliance instructions.
Any electrical power system, control panel, grounding/bonding system,
disconnect, overcurrent protection, emergency-stop power circuit, machine
guarding, facility connection, inspection, certification, field evaluation, or
AHJ review must be designed, built, inspected, and accepted by qualified
professionals for the specific installation and intended environment.

## No structural use without separate project review

M3-CRETE does not certify, approve, warrant, or validate any printed concrete,
mortar, paste, structure, component, repair, form, mold, or construction method.
No printed output from an M3-CRETE-derived system may be used for structural,
load-bearing, life-safety, building-code, transportation, water-control,
pressure-containing, occupied, public-access, or permanent construction use
unless separately designed, tested, permitted, and approved by the responsible
licensed professionals and authorities for that specific project.

## User responsibility

Users and integrators are responsible for the workspace, guarding, personal
protective equipment, lockout/tagout procedures, operator training, material
handling, machine commissioning, professional electrical review, local-code
review, and any project-specific engineering or authority review.

This notice is a public project-scope boundary. It is not legal advice,
electrical engineering approval, product-safety certification, or authority
having jurisdiction approval.
