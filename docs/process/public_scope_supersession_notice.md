# Public Scope Supersession Notice

Status: Draft for counsel and qualified electrical/control-panel review.

The public M3-CRETE scope has been narrowed to mechanical design information,
low-voltage control-interface references, and software/firmware references
where published.

Prior public control-panel examples, planner outputs, default netlists, photos,
screenshots, or prototype references are superseded. They are not validated,
not certified, not wiring instructions, not construction guidance, and not a
basis for energizing or operating any M3-CRETE-derived system.

The public M3-CRETE repository does not provide AC mains wiring instructions,
control-panel build instructions, facility-connection instructions,
safety-rated emergency-stop circuit designs, 48 V high-current power
distribution, or code-compliance instructions.

Any electrical power system, control panel, grounding/bonding system,
disconnect, overcurrent protection, emergency-stop power circuit, machine
guarding, facility connection, inspection, certification, field evaluation, or
AHJ review must be designed, built, inspected, and accepted by qualified
professionals for the specific installation and intended environment.
